Teaching Support for 1st to 7th std

Are you interested in teaching basic English and Math to primary students? We are looking for volunteers who are keen on making a difference to a child's education.

Details

Spark-A-Change Foundation (SAC) believes that "Quality education is every child’s right regardless of socio-Economic strata.Quality education is every child’s right regardless of Socio-Economic strata."

We empower low-income children through joyful interactions with educated adults to foster transformational change, imparting knowledge, values, skills, and support for their academic and personal success.

Are you interested in teaching basic English and Math to primary students? We are looking for volunteers who are keen on making a difference to a child's education.

Join hands with SAC that will be a transformational learning experience that will enable you to Spark A Change to a child's education.

Requirements
  • Age Minimum: 18+
  • Interest in community service
Additional Information

This is a great opportunity to meet other like-minded individuals and enjoy social service

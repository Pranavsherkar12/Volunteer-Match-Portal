Opportunity Details

Volunteer Teacher Required for Community Center

Join Parivartan Sheel Sanstha and become a Volunteer Teacher at our community center in Lokhandwala Kandivali!

Details

Parivartan Sheel Sanstha, established in 2000, empowers communities in India through various social initiatives. They tackle educational disparities by providing scholarships, after-school programs, and workshops. Healthcare services, awareness campaigns, and promoting healthy lifestyles are some ways they improve community health. Additionally, they actively protect the environment through awareness campaigns and sustainable practices. With a proven track record, they've supported over 10,000 children, built 50+ schools, organized health camps, and planted trees.

Here's what you need to know:

  • Role: Volunteer Teacher
  • Mission: Support students academically
  • Location: Lokhandwala Kandivali
  • Benefits: Make a difference in students' lives, contribute to your community, and gain valuable teaching experience
Requirements
  • Age Minimum: 18+
  • Time Commitment: 2 hours per day, 6 days a week, from 2 pm to 6 pm
  • Interest in community service
Additional Information

This is a great opportunity for social service.

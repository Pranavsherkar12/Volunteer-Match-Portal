Opportunity Details

Food Drive Assistance

Help us collect and organize food donations for families in need.

Details

Help us collect and organize food donations for families in need. Volunteers will assist with sorting, packing, and distributing food supplies.

Requirements
  • Availability during weekend drives
  • Ability to lift light boxes
  • Interest in community service
Additional Information

This is a great opportunity to meet other like-minded individuals and enjoy the outdoors. Our team leaders will provide more instructions on the day of the event.

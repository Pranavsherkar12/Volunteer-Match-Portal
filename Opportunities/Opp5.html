Volunteerng for Social Media Analyst

Are you good at social media management? If yes, We are Looking for Social media manager.

Details

WHY VOLUNTEER AT JAAGO BHARAT?

  1. Will get to know many innovations & techniques regarding environmental issues.
  2. Will have a chance to meet many experienced professionals who are experts in this field.
  3. It will help in enhancing your management, communication, negotiations & leadership skills.
  4. In our organization we promote new ideas & leadership, so we gave an equal opportunity to everyone.
  5. Get a Certificate of internship or Letter of Recommendation by fulfilling our criteria and rules & regulations.

TASK TO BE PERFORMED

  1. To execute Digital Intelligence Reports (Social Media Platforms, Online data) by compiling, analyzing, and interpreting data & generating reports and presentations.
  2. Read / Analyses of data from dashboards to provide brand insights.
  3. Support Social Media Managers in understanding followers behavior for focused content marketing strategies
  4. Benchmarking against market leaders & tracking trends
  5. Creating and presenting information in the form of decks to stakeholders

WHO CAN APPLY?

  1. Anyone above 21 years of age who has either worked in an NGO or done any social work.
  2. Responsible person who is capable of dealing with all the issues regarding his team despite maintaining the deadline of his project.
  3. Who has a genuine interest and keen mind regarding social & environmental issues.
  4. Effective communication skills and the ability to communicate issues and challenges with accuracy and concision in oral and written media
  5. Resilient self-starter with a bias for action and possesses a structured approach to problem-solving
Requirements
  • Age Minimum: 18+
  • Interest in community service
Additional Information

This is a great opportunity to meet other like-minded individuals and enjoy social service.
